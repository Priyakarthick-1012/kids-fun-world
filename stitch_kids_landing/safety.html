Frequently Asked

Is outside food allowed? expand_more

Small snacks and baby food are welcome! For full meals, we have wonderful picnic areas just outside the main gates. Glass containers are not permitted inside the park for safety reasons.

Where can I rent a stroller? expand_more

Single and double strollers are available for rent at the Guest Services center near the main entrance. They are first-come, first-served.

What if my child gets lost? expand_more

Don't panic! Alert the nearest staff member immediately. Our "Lost Parents" center is located next to First Aid, where our team is trained to reunite families quickly.
